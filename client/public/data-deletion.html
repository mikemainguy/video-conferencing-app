Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Our video conferencing application is designed so that we do not store any user data beyond what is necessary for the operation of the service.

No Persistent User Data

  • We do not store your personal information, chat history, or video/audio content after your session ends.
  • Authentication information (such as your name or email from Google or Facebook) is used only for the duration of your session and is not retained.
  • Temporary data required for the operation of the service is deleted as soon as it is no longer needed.

How to Request Data Deletion

Since we do not store any persistent user data, there is no data to delete. If you have any concerns or believe that your data has been stored in error, please contact us at support@example.com and we will promptly investigate and address your request.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our data deletion practices, please contact us at support@example.com.
